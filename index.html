Beneficiary Help

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

This service allows a Medicare beneficiaries to import information held by CMS into an application of her or his choice. This service is in pilot mode. We will issue invite codes and are working to make the service open to everyone.

We take security seriously. You must choose to link the Blue Button API to your MyMedicare account. Once you have made the connection you can then choose to connect any of a growing library of personal health applications which can help you manage your health more easily.
